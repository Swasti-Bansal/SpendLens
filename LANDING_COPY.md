## FAQ

**Q: Is this actually free?**
Yes. No credit card, no signup, no catch. SpendLens is a free tool.

**Q: How accurate are the savings estimates?**
All pricing data is pulled directly from vendor pricing pages and verified weekly. Savings calculations are based on plan-fit rules — if your team size doesn't need Business features, we flag it.

**Q: Do you store my data?**
Your tool and spend data stays in your browser. We only store your email if you choose to submit it.

**Q: Who is this for?**
Startup founders, engineering managers, and CTOs who pay for AI tools but have never audited whether they're on the right plan.

**Q: What tools do you support?**
Cursor, GitHub Copilot, Claude, ChatGPT, Anthropic API, OpenAI API, Gemini, and Windsurf — covering the most common AI tools used by engineering teams.
