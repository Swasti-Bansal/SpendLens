# Economics

## What a Converted Lead is Worth to Credex

Credex sells discounted AI credits at roughly 20-40% below retail. A company spending $1,000/mo on AI tools represents $200-400/mo in potential Credex revenue if they switch their purchasing.

Assume average Credex customer spends $800/mo on credits with 30% margin = $240/mo gross profit per customer. Annual value per customer = $2,880.

SpendLens itself is the CAC reduction mechanism — Credex acquires leads for free through a tool that provides genuine value first.

## Conversion Funnel

| Stage | Rate | Notes |
|-------|------|-------|
| Visitor → Audit completed | 60% | Form is simple, no friction |
| Audit completed → Email submitted | 25% | Value shown before ask |
| Email submitted → Credex consultation booked | 8% | Only high-savings leads contacted |
| Consultation → Credit purchase | 35% | Warm lead, specific savings identified |

Per 1,000 visitors:
- 600 complete audit
- 150 submit email
- 12 book consultation (high-savings filter)
- 4 purchase credits

Revenue per 1,000 visitors = 4 × $2,880 annual value = $11,520

## What Would Drive $1M ARR in 18 Months

$1M ARR = ~347 active Credex customers (at $240/mo gross profit each)

To acquire 347 customers via SpendLens funnel:
- Need 347 / 4 per 1,000 visitors = ~87,000 total visitors over 18 months
- ~4,800 visitors/month
- Achievable with consistent Show HN posts, Twitter presence, SEO on "ai tool cost" keywords

Key assumptions that must hold:
1. High-savings audit rate stays above 15%
2. Email capture rate stays above 25%
3. Consultation-to-purchase rate stays above 30%
4. Average customer LTV stays above $2,000 annually

If any of these drop significantly — revisit the audit engine rules or the Credex offer quality.
